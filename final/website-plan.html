Final Project Site Plan

Site Name:Unicon Aluminum and Solar Solution

This company name represent the image of the company. It shows area of specialization of the company which is the installation aluminum and solar systems.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to project the image of this company in the web space and to showcase her areas of specialization. To make larger community know of the company function and bring more customer traffic.

Scenarios:

This site will provide answers to commonly asked question and scenarios like; where to get good aluminum materials for roofing, quality solar materials for installation, where to get qualified solar energy engineers etc.
