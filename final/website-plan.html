Final Project Site Plan

Site Name: Unicon Aluminum and Solar Solution

This company name represent the image of the company. It shows area of specialization of the company which is the installation of aluminum roofs & profiles, and solar systems.

Site Purpose:

Web Presence: The purpose of this site is to project the image of this company in the web space and to showcase her areas of specialization. To make larger community know of the company function and bring more customer traffic.

Scenarios:

The following are some of the possible scenarios;

  • What is the best quality aluminum?
  • What is the cost of my roof?
  • What is the capacity and cost of the solar power generator that will be sufficient for my appliances?
  • How can I get the best solar power generator?

Color Schema:

The following are color scheme picked for this project as they will best represent the company purpose;

  • #4C115F (Purple) - h1 to h6 elements.
  • #CEE9EE (Light-blue) - background color.
  • #500202 (Dark-Red)
  • #1b007a (Blue)
  • #ffffff (White) - action button textt.

Typography:

Below are google fonts family to be used in the site project;

  • Lato+Mono+Trust google font family - for the body text- this will be applied to the paragraph text.
  • Sofia google font family - for h1 to h6 elements - this will be applied to the h1 and h2 css selectors.
